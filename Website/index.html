Welcome to the homepage of POWAM, a website for bringing new people into the video gaming community. Here we encourage a warm and welcoming community which empowers new people interested in video games and builds their confidence in interacting with the established gaming community. While our goal is to assist new gamers, all are welcome here. Feel free to browse our site for new or old game recommendations, look up terminology that you might have heard thrown around, or read up on the latest news on hardware or the industry itself.
