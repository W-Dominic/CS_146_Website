Cool Retro Games

Here are our suggestions for this week:

The Legend of Zelda: A Link to the Past

Summary: The Legend of Zelda: A Link to the Past is an action-adventure game. It was developed and published by Nintendo for the Super Nintendo Entertainment System and released on November 21, 1991. It is the third game in The Legend of Zelda series. It focuses on Link on his journey to save Hyrule and defeat the dark lord Ganon.

Pros and Cons: A Link to the Past is often called the greatest video game of all time. The pros being that there is accomplished audio direction and it is intricately designed. Some people report performance issues on the Nintendo Switch.

Super Mario Bros. 3

Summary: Super Mario Bros. 3 is a platform game that was developed and published by Nintendo. Players control the brothers Mario or Luigi, who have to save Princess Toadstool from Bowser.They defeat enemies by stomping them or by using other items that have powers. Super Mario Bros. 3 introduced many elements that became Mario series staples.

Pros and Cons: Super Mario Bros. 3 is fantastic in terms of aesthetics and design. It is often called the grandfather of modern gaming. Users have offered little to no cons about this game, but some say there are a few hardware limitations.

Street Fighter II Turbo: Hyper Fighting

Summary: Street Fighter II Turbo: Hyper Fighting is a competitive fighting game that was released in 1992. It is the third game in the Street Fighter II sub-series. This game introduced a faster playing speed and new moves for some of the characters.

Pros and Cons: The pros of Hyper Fighting are that there is a faster gameplay compared to the others in the series and that there are additional moves and options. A con can also be the faster gameplay because it rewuires faster reactions and several users did not like the colors.

Sonic the Hedgehog 2

Summary: Sonic the Hedgehog 2 was developed and published by Sega for the Sega Genesis in 1992. It is the second entry in the Sonic the Hedgehog series. In this entry, Sonic's sidekick, Miles "Tails" Prower, was introduced, and can be controlled by a second player. Sonic and Tails must stop the main antagonist Dr. Ivo Robotnik from stealing the Chaos Emeralds.

Pros and Cons: The pros of Sonic the Hedgehog 2 are the plot and the option to play, as well as the variety of levels. The cons are that some of the levels are too easy to beat.

Super Mario Kart

Summary: Super Mario Kart is a 1992 kart racing video game that was published by Nintendo for the Super Nintendo Entertainment System video game console. It sold 8.76 million copies worldwide. Nintendo re-released it in the US in 2017 as part of the company's Super NES Classic Edition. The player takes control of one of eight Mario series characters that all have different abilities.

Pros and Cons: The main pros of the Super Mario Kart game is the nostalgia and the aesthetics. The cons of this game are the graphics and the simple courses.
